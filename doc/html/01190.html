Remarks

The caller should not use the constant expressions assigned to any of the enumeration constants as these may vary between devices. 

Port pins shown here are representative only. Not all ports pins are available on all devices. Refer to the specific device data sheet to determine which port pins are supported. The MHC will generate this enumeration with the port pins that are available on the device.
